Juan Samudio (@samussphoto) is a photographer and videographer currently residing in Bogota (Colombia) and constantly traveling to LA. He is an enthusiastic and passionate young talent who keeps learning from those he admires while sharing his own personal touch and ideas in his work.

At the age of 22, Juan is making a name for himself in the photography world. He enjoys working with others as he finds fruitful the collaborative and learning processes. Supporting and building each other up is very important for him in order to bring creative ideas to life.
